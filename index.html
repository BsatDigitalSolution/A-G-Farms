Frequently Asked Questions

What types of birds do you supply? +

We supply a wide range of poultry including broilers, layers, noilers, cockerels, and both local and foreign turkeys. All our birds are healthy, vaccinated, and ready for various farming purposes.

Do you provide delivery services? +

Yes, we provide reliable delivery services within Ogun State and neighboring areas. Delivery charges depend on distance and order size. Contact us for specific delivery arrangements.

What is included in your poultry pen setup service? +

Our pen setup service includes custom design, construction, ventilation systems, feeding and watering systems, and biosecurity measures. We provide complete turnkey solutions tailored to your specific needs and budget.

How can I place an order? +

You can place orders through our website, call us directly, or visit our farm location. We recommend calling ahead for large orders to ensure availability. Payment methods include cash, bank transfer, and mobile money.

Do you offer farm management training? +

Yes, we provide comprehensive farm management training covering bird care, disease prevention, feeding strategies, and business management. Training can be conducted at our facility or your location.
